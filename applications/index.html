No matter what the project we have a team of programmers and designers in place to create a powerful site engineered around your specific workflow. Some examples of our Applications are:

  • Real Estate Listings Management - Our realty sites are developed around ease of use. Ease of updating listings allows you to keep your information consistently up to date, and ease of use for your clients to search and view listings in an easy and effective manner.
    • Multiple Picture Functionality
    • Integrated Virtual Tours
    • Mapquest Mapping to properties
    • Integrated Financial Calculators
    • Updateable Pages like news…
    • Capture leads with Contact Submit forms, or register to view listings
  • E-Commerce Development - Coastal Web Technologies makes ecommerce easy and affordable. We will guide you through every aspect of setting up your online store, including:
    • Customer Convenience
      • Customer can view and modify their personal info by logging on to their account. Account can be created before or during CheckOut process.
      • Customer receives confirmation eMails when they place the order, and when their order's status changes.
      • "Email A Friend" functionality allows customer to send a link to a Product via email.
      • Advanced search function.
    • Catalog & Products
      • Item discounts based on quantity ordered.
      • Order discounts with single or multi use discount codes.
      • Group products so that they are displayed together on the product detail page.
      • Store can be run in "Catalog Only" mode. This way, the store acts just like an online catalog, without any links to a shopping cart.
      • Dynamically create product listings on your "Home Page" by setting product(s) to "Featured".
      • Support for product Ratings & Reviews per product.
    • Administration
      • Easily manage Products, Categories, Options, Customers, Shipping, Locations, Orders, etc
      • Upload Product Images and Software Items from your browser to your web server.
      • Configure sales tax per state or country (billing or shipping address).
      • Manage your orders from an intuitive screen. Order stock levels can be adjusted when the order ships.
      • Statistical Charting tool.
      • Send HTML Newsletters to your customers directly from your store.

    • Edit content as a draft, without affecting the online version.
    • Easily publish the finished content.

Coastal Web Technologies is a web design company focused on integrating powerful business application development into a stylish web presence. We dedicate ourselves to three main goals:

  • Application Development – Our application development expertise make it easy for you to manage your website through a built in password protected administration area. This makes it extremely easy to update the text, events, and real estate listings. Your website will be much more than a pretty picture.
  • User Experience – Your potential clients will have an overwhelming positive view of your organization though the look and effectiveness of your website. Custom one-of-a-kind design will strategically convey your organization’s philosophy and brand to your clients, while powerful application development will enable effective communication of your latest information.
  • Customer Experience – Coastal Web Technologies’ bread and butter is word-of-mouth marketing. We pride ourselves on the fact that our customers tell others about our excellent customer service and communication skills. If you haven’t spoken to any of our existing customers, please contact us and we will put you in touch with a current customer relative to your web design needs.

March 9th, 2006 - Coastal Web 2.0 Launched, Coastal Web Technologies is proud to present a new www.coastalwebtech.com. We have been so busy over the last six months that upkeep on our own site and marketing collateral has slipped by the wayside. In the last two month’s however, we have hired two very talented designers to let us come up for air and are now ready to present our new site to the public. We feel that our site now effectively communicates our talents, and showcases our current projects.

A main goal of the new site is to provide current information on projects we have recently finished and news about our growth in North Florida. Please check back frequently to see the latest sites and development projects.

March 5th, 2006 – Coastal Web Partners with The St. Augustine Record. Coastal Web Tech is exited to announce a partnership with the St. Augustine Record. The Record has asked Coastal Web to engineer an iFrame Surf Report for The Record’s new website. The development of the iFrame required engineering a database driven webpage that interfaces directly with the surf report database in real time. Take a look at http://www.staugustine.com/surfreport.shtml to see the end results… The site should launch soon!!!

February 15th, 2006 – Come on By! Today was a big day for Coastal Web Technologies, after a year and a half of working remotely, Coastal is proud to announce it has opened its new office doors at 1095 Anastasia Blvd. The office represents the corporate headquarters for Coastal, where Mike Herchel and Erik Antonson will run Coastal Web Tech. Come on by and say hello the next time you are in the area! Thank you for the support St. Augustine.

January 13th, 2006 – Coastal Hires new Flash Animator and Web Designer. Welcome aboard James Russell. Coastal Web is enthused to announce that we have hired a new Flash Animator and Web Designer James Russell to the team. James is a welcome addition as his talents in design and flash are a nice compliment to our established expertise in database and complex web development.
